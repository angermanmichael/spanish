

### Endings of Simple Tenses of the Indicative

##### Present

| ar | er | ir |
|-|-|-|
| o , amos | o, emos | o, imos |
| as | es | es |
| a, an | e, en | e, en |

##### Imperfect used to, was

| ar | er, ir |
|-|-|
| aba, ábamos | ía, íamos |
| abas  | ías |
| aba, aban | ía, ían |

##### Preterite did

| ar | er, ir |
|-|-|
| é, amos | í, imos |
| aste  | íste |
| ó, aron | ío, ieron |

##### Future shall, will

| ar, er, ir |
|-|
| é, emos |
| ás  |
| á, án |

##### Conditional would

| ar, er, ir |
|-|
| ía, íamos |
| ía  |
| ía, ian |


##### verb synopsis

| indicative | tense | english |
|-|-|-|
| present | hablo | I speak |
| imperfect | hablaba | I used to speak |
| preterite | hablé | I spoke |
| future | hablaré | I shall speak |
| conditional | hablaría | I would speak |
